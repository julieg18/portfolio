Hi! I’m Julie, and I started learning to code shortly after I graduated from high school at the age of 17. As soon as I started to make my first HTML websites, I knew this was something I wanted to do for the rest of my life. I’ve been studying web development ever since.

Over the years, I’ve studied both front-end web development with JavaScript and back-end web development with NodeJS. While I do enjoy both sides of web developent, I prefer the front-end over the back-end. I love taking a website design, figuring out what kind of web technologies are needed, and turning it into a fully functional site with code!
